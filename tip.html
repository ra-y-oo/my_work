Tip Calculator App

A tip calculator app built with HTML, CSS and JavaScript. A practical and user-friendly tool designed to help individuals quickly and accurately calculate the appropriate tip amount for a bill at a restaurant, cafe, or any other service-based establishment. It provides a simple interface for users to enter the bill amount, select a tip percentage, and calculate the tip and total amounts.

During development I learnt Responsive Design to ensure that the app adapts seamlessly to different resolutions, orientations, and aspect ratios.
